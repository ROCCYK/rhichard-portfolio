About me

Welcome! My name is Rhichard Koh. I am from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. During my senior year of high school I was introduced to trading, I then quickly got interested in trading currency because of the high leverage and earning potential. After graduating high school I attended Online Trading Academy to properly learn how to trade with proper risk management, technical analysis, as well as fundamental analysis.

After quickly learning the concepts, the instructors noticed and offered me the role of becoming a Student Support Specialist. I accepted the offer and helped teach the students proper risk management, technical analysis, and fundamental analysis. I got to meet many hedge fund traders, independent traders, and institutional traders. They informed me about where trading is going in the near future and introduced me to quantitive trading.

I became interested in becoming a quantitive trader. Diving into that topic I learned about machine learning and deep learning and I got passionate about learning about artificial intelligence. I am now in Durham College for Honours Bachelor’s of Artificial Intelligence, aspiring to become a machine learning engineer.
