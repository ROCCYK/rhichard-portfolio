About me

Welcome! My name is Rhichard Koh. I am from Toronto, Ontario, Canada. During the senior year of highscool I was introduced to trading, I then quickly got interested in trading currency because of the high leverage and earning potential. After graduating highschool I attened Online Trading Academy to properly learn how to trade with proper risk management, technical analysis, as well as fundamental analysis.

After quickly learning the concepts, the instructors noticed and offered me the role of becoming a Student Support Specialist. I accepted the offer and helped teach the students proper risk management, technical analysis, and fundamental analysis. I got to meet many hedge fund traders, independant traders, and institutional traders. They informed me where trading is going in the near future and introduced me into quantitive trading.

I became interested in becoming a quantitive trader. Diving into that topic I learned about machine learning and deep learning and I got passionate learning about artificial intelligence. I am now in Durham College for Honours Bachelor’s of Artificial Intelligence, aspiring to become a machine learning engineer.
